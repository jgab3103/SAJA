SAJA

Sally Jamila and Jamie Gabriel are artists based in Auckland and Sydney. Their projects blend music and technology in a cultural exploration, deeply inspired by the paradoxes of the human condition and the greater spiritual reality. Through their multidisciplinary practices, they reflect diverse interests that range from sound design to improvisation to spiritual practice.

Spun Treasures

SAJA are releasing a fresh collection of works that explore folk songs linked to Sally Jamila’s genetic and cultural lineage. Born in Aotearoa, Sally’s Cook Island-Tahitian roots are here interwoven with her Croatian and Indian heritage, providing a rich blend of influences. Their eclectic, multi-lingual repertoire spins folkloric treasure into new offerings. Sally’s rich and sensual voice carries these threads with soul, in effortless conversation with Jamie’s vibrant sonic tapestry on acoustic guitar.
